What the Numbers Tell Us

  • The residential air purifier market is expanding rapidly, nearly doubling from 2020 to 2023.
  • Philips India grew its personal health revenue even during COVID, showing brand resilience.
  • Despite strong product performance, profits declined — indicating cost pressure and competition.
  • Philips sits in a mid-premium zone: better performance than budget brands, cheaper than Dyson.
  • The core challenge is not product capability, but seasonal demand and low pan-India awareness.
